Compares 13 free LLM API platforms including OpenRouter, Google AI Studio, Groq, and Mistral, examining rate limits, models, context windows, and hidden costs like data-training policies. Highlights Groq's LPU hardware running Llama 3.3 70B at around 320 tokens per second, Google AI Studio's up to 1 million token context, and Mistral's roughly 1 billion free tokens monthly with data-training opt-in; OpenRouter offers one OpenAI-compatible endpoint for 20+ free models.
